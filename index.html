Instructions

Welcome to TheMagicTrick.net

Our App isn’t technically a trick.

It’s more of a prop intended to be simple enough to be used by beginners to perform a very simple but powerful effect or incorporated into routines by the most advanced conjurers.

The effect is simple:
  • The mark visits www.TheMagicTrick.net and selects a playing card from a standard deck of cards.
  • You are then able to mysteriously identify the card they chose.

Important - Please read before downloading!

To perform the trick you will need the following items:
  • Android device that is compatible with the App
  • A windows computer with:
    • Flash enabled (This is a standard plug-in on computers and is essential to run streaming video pages such as YouTube)
    • Javascript enabled – As standard this is enabled but some people may opt to switch java script off.
    • Cookies enabled – the effect is achieved by pairing the computer and the android phone using a temporary cookie.
To check for computer compatibility we have created a webpage that will test for the required items automatically – simply visit www.TheMagicTrick.net/test.

Performing the trick is simple:
  1. Click and hold the top hat logo for two seconds, until a code number appears.
    If you can't long-click, click on the tiniest star on the hat logo.
  2. Open the App on your phone and enter the code.
  3. Select Simple or Dark mode.
  4. Position the phone so you can see it but the mark cannot.
  5. The mark clicks on a card.
  6. You correctly identify the card selected.

Hints and Tips

  • Switch off the ringer on your phone!
  • As with all good tricks, do not over do it. Repeating a trick will make it more obvious or give the mark more chances to catch you out.
  • With the low contrast 'Dark' mode enabled you may wish to have the phone in plain sight and boldly predict what they have picked with a little misdirection.
  • Ensure the line of sight to the phone is easy for you to view but difficult for the mark to see, a smaller viewing angle can be achieved by placing the phone next to something.
  • Practice standing and performing the trick so the mark will not move and see the phone.
  • As with all good tricks practice showmanship and spin a story round the effect so it’s more impressive.
  • If people know that a smartphone is involved then the trick is simple to figure out – do not give away that a phone is used!
  • For the more advanced magicians out there please feel free to send us feedback or ideas for other apps that might be useful.
  • The code cookie lasts 48 hours, so you have plenty of time to prep the mark's computer in advance.
